Poker Variants

HOLD'em

Each players is given two cards. They have to make the best five-cards combination by using three other cards from the centre of the poker table.

OMAHA HiLO

Omaha poker, players here compete for both halves of the bot. It is generally played with fixed betting.

PLO

In PLO, the betting amount is already decided and one can't bet more than the limit of the pot. The amount of the pot is the reward.
